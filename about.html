About Tiny AI World

A personal platform for exploring AI, Machine Learning, and beyond.

üåç What is This?

Tiny AI World is an interactive platform that visualizes the connections between different topics in AI, machine learning, programming, and data science. Unlike traditional note-taking apps, this platform shows how concepts relate to each other, making it easier to understand the bigger picture.

Every node in the graph represents a topic, and the connections show how these topics are related. Click on any node to dive deeper into that subject.

üéØ The Mission

My goal is to create a free, open-source learning resource that helps people understand complex AI/ML concepts through connected, bite-sized notes. Knowledge is better when it's shared, and learning is more effective when you can see the relationships between ideas.
